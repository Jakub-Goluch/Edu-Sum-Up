## Podsumowanie:
Thucydides emphasized the systemic perspective on the clash between Athenian and Spartan interests, highlighting the power transition and other factors leading to war. Osiander (2007) provided a corrected translation of key passages, emphasizing Athenians as the primary cause of the war due to their actions, leaving no alternative to conflict.

In Thucydides' analysis of the Athenian-Spartan conflict, he focused on various factors beyond just power transition that led to war. Osiander (2007) supported this view by offering a corrected translation that highlighted Athenians as the main instigators of the war through their actions, challenging previous interpretations and emphasizing the agency of the Athenians in precipitating the conflict.
